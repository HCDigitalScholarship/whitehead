About the Curators

The material available on this webpage was prepared by student curator, Carmen Ana (Nana) Nieto. Nana is a rising Junior from Guaynabo, Puerto Rico. She’s focusing on her Museum Studies minor and History of Art major at Bryn Mawr College, and hopes to continue doing work related to exhibition design, arts management and visitor engagement in museum spaces. Whenever she’s not missing her dogs or FaceTiming her family, she enjoys exercising regularly, meeting new people, exploring different cities and listening to music.

Curator note

I would like to thank O'Donnell and McCausland families for their generous support on behalf of their father, Joseph O’Donnell. The Joseph E. O’Donnell Research Internship experience has prepared me for for exhibition timelines and best research and writing practices for text on display. I am very grateful that I got the opportunity to research John Whitehead and learn from his passionate life’s work.
